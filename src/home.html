Bionic reading is an effective method for reading and processing text. This method involves making the first few letters of a word bold, forcing your brain to fill in the rest of the word. The notes feature would allow users to write notes whilst browsing a website. This feature could be particularly useful for websites that contain a lot of important information that needs to be memorised. Studies have shown that people with ADHD tend to have issues with their working memory therefore, having a space where they can make notes is essential.

Studies have shown that those with ADHD are able to read more efficiently if some parts of a text is highlighted. An article published in the Journal of Abnormal Child Psychology suggested that those with ADHD were able to focus better on a text if it had an element of novelty to it. Hence, implementing a highlight feature would allow users to highlight appropriate areas of text that could then make the text easier to process.
